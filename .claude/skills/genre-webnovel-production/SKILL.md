# Genre Webnovel Production

## Principle

A web novel chapter is a promise machine: clear want, fast pressure, emotional turn, and a reason to open the next episode.

## Chapter Beat

1. Re-anchor the protagonist's current want.
2. Put a practical obstacle on the page within the first scene.
3. Add a relationship or status cost.
4. Reveal one useful clue.
5. End on a changed question, not a random shock.

## Genre Dials

| Genre | Dial |
| --- | --- |
| Romance fantasy | Polite distance, status power, emotional misread |
| Modern fantasy | Everyday place, hidden rule, sudden cost |
| Thriller | Evidence pressure, compression, compromised choice |
| SF | Scale, systems, political or technical consequence |
| Literary | Recurring image, interior contradiction, restrained reveal |

## Output Contract

- chapter hook
- 4 to 6 outline beats
- draft prose
- cliffhanger sentence
- retention note

Do not invent continuity to create drama. Ask the continuity editor for a repair when the desired scene conflicts with canon.
